A Simple Bookmark Manager

A clean and simple interface to organize your favourite websites. Open a new browser tab and see your sites load instantly. Try it for free.

Features

Our aim to make it quick and easy for you to access your favourite websites. Your bookmarks sync between your devices so you can access them on the go.

  • Simple Bookmarking
  • Speady Searching
  • Easy Sharing

Bookmark in one click

Organize your bookmarks however you like. Our simple drag-and-drop interface gives you complete control over how you manage your favourite sites.

Intelligent Searching

Our powerful search feature will help you find saved sites in no time at all. No need to trwal through all of your bookmarks.

Share your bookmarks

Easily share your bookmarks and collections with others. Create shareable link that you can send at the click of a button.
